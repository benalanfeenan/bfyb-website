Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and services

Do your prices include GST?

Yes, all prices quoted are inclusive of GST. There are no hidden charges or additional fees beyond what we quote you in writing.

Do you offer discounts for multiple services?

Yes, we offer package discounts when you combine multiple services. Contact us for a custom quote on combined pest control, carpet cleaning and restoration services.

Is there a travel charge?

For water restoration, travel is charged at $1.70/km and $84/hr technician time, up to 45km from Armidale CBD. For pest control and carpet cleaning within our standard service area, there is typically no extra travel charge.

What are the minimum fees?

Pest control has a minimum fee of $182, carpet cleaning $289, and upholstery cleaning $289. The exception is Flea End-of-Lease with Carpet ($157) which is exempt from the minimum when a bundle discount is active.

What do the carpet condition multipliers mean?

We assess your carpet condition on arrival. Bronze (1.0x) is for minimal staining at the base room rate. Silver (1.30x) is for heavy soiling, staining, or pet stains with odour. Gold (2.11x) is a restoration-level clean for extremely stained carpets. Your technician will assess and confirm before starting work.

What areas do you service?

We provide services to Armidale, Uralla, Guyra, Black Mountain, Walcha and surrounding areas in the New England Region. Check our locations page for details or call for service availability in your area.

Do you have emergency services?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency services for flood restoration and water damage. Call 02 5765 0065 for immediate assistance with any emergency situation.
