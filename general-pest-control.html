General Pest Control FAQs

How long does a general pest control treatment take? ▼
Most general pest control treatments take 2-4 hours depending on the size and complexity of your property. Our technician will assess your specific situation and provide a timeframe when arriving. You'll be able to re-enter your home safely after the chemicals have dried, typically 2-3 hours.
Is the pest control treatment safe for my family and pets? ▼
Yes, absolutely. We use low-toxicity, professional-grade treatments that are safe for families and pets once dry. We provide clear instructions before treatment about vacating the property and re-entry times. Children and pets can return after the recommended drying period (typically 2-3 hours). Always follow our post-treatment care instructions for optimal safety and effectiveness.
What pests does general pest control cover? ▼
Our general pest spray covers all common household pests including cockroaches, spiders, ants, silverfish, and other general crawling insects. It's our most popular service because it addresses the majority of pest problems in Armidale homes. For specialized treatments like termites, fleas, or bedbugs, we offer dedicated services — call us to discuss your specific pest concerns.
What does the 12-month warranty cover? ▼
Our 12-month warranty guarantees that if any of the pests covered by your general pest spray treatment return within 12 months, we'll retreat your property at no charge. This gives you complete peace of mind. Simply call us if you notice pests returning and we'll schedule a free follow-up treatment.
