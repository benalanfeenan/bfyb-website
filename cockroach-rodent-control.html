Cockroach & Rodent Control — Frequently Asked Questions

How long does cockroach and rodent treatment take? ▼
A standard cockroach or rodent treatment takes 45-60 minutes. Multiple visits may be needed for complete elimination, depending on infestation severity.
Are your treatments safe for families and pets? ▼
Yes. We use family and pet-friendly products. We recommend staying out of treated areas for 2-3 hours until dry, then it's completely safe.
How much does cockroach and rodent control cost? ▼

What warranty do you provide for cockroach and rodent control? ▼
