Termite Inspections Armidale — Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a termite inspection cost in Armidale? ▼
Termite inspections start from $249 for standard residential properties. We provide free quotes so you know the exact cost before we start. Pre-purchase inspections may cost more depending on property size and complexity.
What is thermal imaging and why is it used in termite inspections? ▼
Thermal imaging detects heat signatures that reveal areas of termite activity and moisture problems behind walls and in hidden spaces without causing damage. This advanced technology allows us to find termite colonies you can't see with the naked eye, making it essential for comprehensive inspections.
How long does a termite inspection take? ▼
A standard termite inspection takes 1-2 hours depending on property size. Large commercial properties or rural properties may require additional time. We'll give you a time estimate when you book.
What should I do if termites are found during the inspection? ▼
We'll provide a detailed written report with photos and recommendations. We can discuss treatment options including chemical barriers, baiting systems, and prevention measures tailored to your property. If you'd like, we can provide a separate quote for treatment.
