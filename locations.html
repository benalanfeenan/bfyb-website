Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service areas and coverage

Do you service my suburb or postcode? ▼
We service all areas listed on our locations page (Armidale, Uralla, Guyra, Walcha, Glen Innes, Black Mountain, and Hillgrove). However, if your area isn't listed, don't assume we can't help! We regularly expand our coverage and often service properties in surrounding areas. Call us on 02 5765 0065 to confirm service availability for your specific address.
What's the difference between same-day, next-day, and scheduled service? ▼
Same-Day Service: We'll visit your property on the same day you call (where possible), typically within 2-3 hours of your request.

Next-Day Service: We'll schedule a visit for the following business day after your call.

Scheduled Service: Certain areas are serviced on set days (e.g., Walcha on Tuesdays & Thursdays). We'll schedule your service for the next available scheduled day. Emergency calls are accommodated where possible.
How do I book a service for a location outside of Armidale? ▼
Simply use our online quote form to describe your service needs and provide your address. Alternatively, call us on 02 5765 0065 and we'll discuss your requirements and confirm availability. We'll let you know the expected timeframe for your service and any scheduling details specific to your location.
Is there an additional charge for traveling to distant locations? ▼
We offer transparent pricing across all our service areas. While we may include a travel component in quotes for locations further from Armidale, our pricing is competitive and reflects the true cost of service. We'll provide a detailed quote upfront so there are no surprises. See our Pricing page for more details.
What if I have an emergency outside of standard hours? ▼
We understand that pest emergencies, flooding, and other property crises don't follow business hours. Call us on 02 5765 0065 and our team will advise on emergency availability. While same-day response may not always be possible for distant locations at night, we'll do everything we can to prioritize urgent situations and provide advice until we can attend.
Can you service rural properties or acreage outside town boundaries? ▼
Yes! We service rural properties and acreage throughout the New England region. Rural pest control, termite inspections, and flood restoration are all services we regularly provide for farms and rural residential properties. Call us with your address and we'll confirm coverage. Some rural locations may be scheduled based on our routing efficiency, but we're experienced with property types across the entire region.
Do you offer pest control in all Armidale suburbs? ▼
Yes, we cover all Armidale suburbs including Central, East, West, South Armidale, UNE Campus, and Dumaresq. Same-day service guaranteed. View Armidale service area.
