Is your pest spray safe for kids and pets? ▼
Yes. We use family and pet-friendly products. We recommend staying out of treated areas for 2-3 hours until dry, then it's completely safe.
How long does a pest treatment take? ▼
A standard home treatment takes 45-60 minutes. Termite inspections take 1-2 hours depending on property size.
Do I need to leave the house during treatment? ▼
We recommend staying out of treated areas for 2-3 hours. You don't need to leave the property entirely.
What's included in a termite inspection? ▼
A thorough inspection of all accessible areas using thermal imaging and moisture meters. You receive a detailed written report within 24-48 hours.

We recommend annual treatments for general pests. Termite inspections should be done annually. High-risk properties may benefit from 6-monthly treatments.
Do you offer emergency pest control? ▼
Yes, we offer same-day emergency service for urgent pest issues. Call us and we'll prioritise your job.
What warranty do you provide? ▼
