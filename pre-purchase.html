Frequently Asked Questions

What does a pre-purchase inspection cover? +
A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection covers all areas of the property including the interior, exterior, subfloor, roof void, and all structural elements. We check for termites, other pests, moisture issues, wood decay, fungal damage, and any structural concerns that could affect the property's safety or value.
How long does the inspection take? +
Inspection duration depends on the type of report and property size. Timber pest inspections (AS 4349.3) typically take 1-2 hours, comprehensive pest reports take 2-3 hours, and combined building & pest reports take 3-4 hours. Our inspector will work efficiently while ensuring a thorough examination.
How quickly do I get the report? +
We deliver detailed reports within 24-48 hours of your inspection. This fast turnaround ensures your property purchase timeline stays on track. Reports are delivered digitally for immediate access.
Can I attend the inspection? +
Yes, we strongly recommend being present during the inspection. This allows you to ask questions directly, see any issues firsthand, and gain a better understanding of the property's condition. However, if you can't be present, we can arrange access with your real estate agent or the property owner.
What if termites or other pests are found? +
Our detailed report documents all findings with photographs and provides treatment recommendations. If pests are found, you can use this information to negotiate with the seller, request they arrange treatment, request a price reduction, or in some cases, withdraw from the purchase depending on your contract terms and the severity of the infestation.
Is this the same as a building inspection? +
No, they're different. A pest inspection focuses on termites, borers, and other pest damage, while a building inspection covers structural issues and overall building condition. Our combined building & pest report covers both areas comprehensively for complete peace of mind.
How much does an inspection cost? +
Timber pest inspections start from $299, comprehensive pest reports from $249, and combined building & pest reports from $499 (all prices include GST). Costs may vary depending on property size and location. For an exact quote, please contact us with your property details.
Do you inspect all areas of the New England region? +
Yes! Bang For Your Buck provides pre-purchase inspections across the New England region including Armidale, Uralla, Guyra, Walcha, Glen Innes, and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm we service your specific location.

What does a pre-purchase property inspection in Armidale include? +
Full building inspection, termite detection with thermal imaging, moisture readings, structural assessment, detailed report emailed within 24 hours.

Strongly recommended. Armidale's climate and older timber homes increase termite risk. Undetected termite damage can cost thousands. Link to pest-control services.
