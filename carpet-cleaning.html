Carpet Cleaning Armidale — Frequently Asked Questions

How long does carpet cleaning take? ▼
Most homes take 2-4 hours depending on size and carpet condition. A typical 3-bedroom home usually takes 2.5-3 hours. We'll provide a time estimate during your free quote. Large commercial spaces may require multiple visits.
How long until carpets are dry? ▼
With our professional drying equipment, most carpets are walk-safe within 2-4 hours. We use high-powered extraction and air movers to accelerate drying. In cooler months or humid conditions, it may take slightly longer. Avoid heavy foot traffic for the first few hours after cleaning.
Is your cleaning safe for kids and pets? ▼
Absolutely! We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning solutions that are completely safe for children and pets. Our products are biodegradable and leave no harmful residue. You can let kids and pets back on the carpet as soon as it's dry.
Will cleaning remove all stains? ▼
Most stains can be removed with our professional stain treatment. However, some permanent dyes or very old stains may not be fully removable without specialist treatment. We'll assess stain type during inspection and provide realistic expectations. If a stain can't be removed, we'll let you know upfront.
How often should I get carpets cleaned? ▼
For most households, professional cleaning every 12-18 months maintains carpet longevity and appearance. High-traffic areas or homes with pets may benefit from annual cleaning. We recommend a deep clean before the peak summer season and after winter when dirt accumulation is highest.
Do you move furniture? ▼
Yes! We move light furniture (chairs, small tables) at no extra charge. For large furniture (couches, heavy cabinets), we may need to work around it or discuss options. Please let us know about furniture arrangements when booking so we can plan accordingly.
What's included in end of lease cleaning? ▼
Our end of lease cleaning includes deep hot water extraction of all carpeted areas, stain removal, odour treatment if needed, and fibre grooming. We include a final walkthrough to ensure everything meets rental return standards. We provide a bond-back guarantee so you get your full security deposit.
Do you clean rugs as well? ▼
Yes! We clean all types of rugs including area rugs, doormats, entrance mats, and specialty rugs. We have the expertise to clean delicate antique rugs, modern synthetic rugs, and everything in between. We can clean rugs on-site or remove them for specialized cleaning if needed.
How much does carpet cleaning cost in Armidale? ▼
Carpet cleaning in Armidale starts from $289 (minimum fee). A typical 3-bedroom home costs between $289-$400 depending on room sizes and carpet condition. We offer free, no-obligation quotes for all Armidale properties. View our full pricing page or call 02 5765 0065.
Do you offer end-of-lease carpet cleaning in Armidale? ▼
Yes, end-of-lease carpet cleaning is one of our most popular services in Armidale, especially around the university rental areas. We provide bond-back guarantee cleaning that meets real estate agent standards. Many Armidale property managers recommend BFYB for their tenants.
How long does it take for carpets to dry after cleaning in Armidale? ▼
Drying time in Armidale typically ranges from 4-8 hours depending on the season. Armidale's cooler climate and lower humidity can actually help with drying in summer, though winter jobs may take slightly longer. We use professional extraction equipment to remove as much moisture as possible during the clean.
